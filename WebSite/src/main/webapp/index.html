IntelliText

A Smart Text Editor, 30 years ahead.

Throughout many years of writing essays a person might come to realize all word processors are all practically the same. They all possess the same features and do the same thing. Not this one. We have come up with ways to optimize the users performance and make the drag of writing essays or notes that much easier.

What makes IntelliText Special

Math

When Listening in a math lecture, often students get lost in the fundamentals of what a teacher says. Now with this feature you can type any function and we will graph it for you, as well as solving equations for you. This way we can all understand math class a little better.

Grammar

When Writing one might forget to find various information to spice up your essay. This feature will read through your writing and find information you might have left out. This way you can consider including it in your paper.

Research

When writing an essay you might forget about the english language. This is where IntelliText comes in, you can type normally and we will find words that are repeated too often, mismatched tenses in a sentence, as well as other useful features. This way you can focus on the writing and leave the grammer to us.

Annotations

When writing notes or an essay annotations could be really useful. You can set off sections in your paper to be titles, body paragraphs, or any custom thing you can imagine. This way in your notes or essays you can mark important points or confusing points to go back to and re-read/write.
